Research Overview

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering has been a recognized Research centre under VTU since the year 2012. The researchers can register for Ph.D programs in the domains of • Wireless Communication • Wireless Communication Networks • Antennas • Mobile Communication • VLSI • Signal Processing etc, with the faculty well vased within there domains. Currently there are about 28 students have registered for Ph.D program and three research Scholar are awarded from Electronics & Communication Engineering Research Center, BIT, Bangalore.
