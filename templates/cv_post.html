POST GRADUATION

Department of Civil Engineering has started this PG Program in the year 2003. The program is approved by AICTE and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Karnataka. The M.Tech (structures) programme is of four semesters (two year) duration. During this tenure, they acquire the in depth knowledge in various fields of Civil Engineering though the rigorous, brain storming teaching- learning process. In addition, the students will be encouraged and motivated to learn the art of effective dissemination of knowledge through seminars and technical paper publications. They are even trained to carryout good project work/dissertation, so that in future, they are enabled to shoulder the responsibility of performing, managing any project efficiently. The special feature of the programme is to develop aspirant students as self-pride, confident, efficient professionals/entrepreneurs/consultants. Students will have 6 months internship programme during third semester and our department center provides facilities for internship recruitments from major companies. Most of the students have bagged internship in very reputed companies and they offered placement in the same.

Facilities

An independent laboratory is provided for PG with three loading frames with testing facilities. Two separate class rooms are provided with department library. Many of the staff members are well qualified with doctoral degree.
