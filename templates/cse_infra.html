Infrastructure and Facilities available:

Department has enough class and tutorial rooms, there are seven UG class rooms,2 PG class room with most of them equipped with projectors, one PG lab and research lab and there are six well equipped laboratories. The library is equipped with books, journals and student project reports, for quick reference for both students and staff.

The Departmental Laboratories - Algorithm & Data Structures Labs, Database & Networking Labs, Web Technologies, System Software & Operating system Labs and the Microprocessor Lab are equipped with state of the art technologies.
