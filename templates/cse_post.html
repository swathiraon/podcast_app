POST GRADUATION

Department of Computer Science and Engineering started PG Program in the year 2008 with intake of 18 students headed by Dr. Shivanand Handigundi. The program is approved by AICTE and is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technology University (VTU), Karnataka. Currently headed by Dr. D G Jyothi.


The M.Tech Programme is of four semester (two years) duration. During this tenure, students acquire in depth knowledge in various fields of Computer Science Engineering like Internet of Things, Big Data, Data Mining, Computer Networks, Storage systems etc using Machine Learning, Web Mining and other techniques. Students are provided with infrastructure and faculty support. Department has well educated and highy experienced staff to train PG students. All 3 rd semester PG students are doing internship in reputed IT companies like DELL, Nokia, Nvest Global Enterprises, DRDO DEBEL, Inventeron Technologies and Business LLP, etc


The program has the credit of getting ranks every year and this year 2 ranks are secured

    1. Harshitha S., 1BI16SCS04 (CGPA: 9.11)
    2. Pooja G. R., 1BI16SCS14 (CGPA: 8.99)

and nearly 90% of PG students are employed. Students are taught how to use peer reviewed journal papers for presenting papers and doing innovative projects. All students do present papers in reputed peer reviewed journals/conferences as part of the academic requirement.

Facilities

PG students will be able to use up-to-date learning resources including a well-stocked library with traditional and electronic media, general purpose network computing facilities and independent laboratory.


The Department is having Research and Development Centre to carry out Research works to acquire Ph.D degree.
