TEDx BIT

TedxBIT Bangalore is a platform to propagate ideas globally through a local platform. Bangalore Institute of Technology holds a university level license from TED to organize an independent TEDx event to effectuate the same. It was initiated by a humble and aspiring group of young visionaries dedicated to cumulate the ideas worth spreading. Orbiting the thoughts to later conceive them to put forth the same, in front of innumerable curious spectators is our primary motive.
