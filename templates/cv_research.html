RESEARCH

Research grants of 9.2 lakhs was sanctioned by Visvesvaraya Technological University to carry out the research work at Bangalore Institute of Technology in Civil Engineering Department research centre for the research work on “An experimental and numerical investigation on the performance evaluation of PSC beams retrofitted by different techniques using artificial and natural fibre reinforced polymers”. Reasearch grant of Rs. 19,50,000 is sanctioned under AICTE MODROBS to principal investigator Dr. K. V. Vijayendra of our department during the year 2018. The department is accredited by National Board of Accreditation.

The department is associated with Karnataka State Pollution Control Board, Indian Concrete Association, INSTRUCT, Indian Green Building Council, Indian Chemical Council, Mumbai, Indian Society of Hospital Waste Management. Internationally BIT’s civil department is associated with the Indian chapter for Academy of certificate Hazardous Material Managers (ACHMM – India).

Civil engineering students have excelled in extracurricular activities and have brought many prizes and awards. Various National and international workshops and conferences were organized by the department in co-ordination with the management of Rajya Vokkaligara Sangha and other bodies.
